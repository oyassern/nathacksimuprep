## Dog Fight
1. Gather the information from the client with the critical dog "Bella"
2. Communicate with the veterinary team members to manage concurrent tasks
3. Practice conflict resolution
4. Gather informed consent
5. Triage and handle routine client inquiries
6. Manage client records and appointments using Cornerstone
7. Administer first aid, assess and begin treatment of critical patient
8. Update/report patient status to client as appropriate

## NAIT Pool: Pediatric Choking/Arrest
1. Demonstrate effective leadership and management of a team (CRM principles) in a pediatric choking child in cardiopulmonary arrest
2. Perform the BLS and ALS treatment of an unconscious FBAO/choking child in cardiac arrest, including the use of Magill forceps
3. Perform the PALS guidelines for a pediatric cardiac arrest including asystole and VF
4. Perform a successful IO including the proper set up of the equipment
5. Discuss the benefit for peer-support and CISM in pediatric population critical events
